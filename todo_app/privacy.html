About Task List 2023 with LocalStorage

This task list application does not save any information onto the cloud (or online), but instead uses your local browser (like Firefox or Chrome) via HTML5 localStorage to save username and task data for later retrieval.
The benefit is that your information remains private to your device, and is never harvested, collected, shared or sold with any party anywhere in person or virtually.

The shortcoming is that (in its current configuration) your information is only available to you in the browser you created the task in, and it does not go from one browser to another, or from one laptop/phone you use to another. In addition, if your browser or device fails, there is no possibility of remote data recovery or retrieval.
